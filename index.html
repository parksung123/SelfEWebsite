Grow with Self-E

Curious about whether a lifestyle change will positively impact your life?

Interested in optimizing your habits to fit your personal needs and abilities?

With Self-E, you can modify your behaviors, report outcomes and gain concrete insights into the efficacy of the self-improvement you seek to make. Through Self-E, you can set up experiments, view your data, and attain individualized insights.

How It Works

Self-E lets you run small experiments on yourself, which may help you optimize various aspects of your well-being.

Sleep

Productivity

Energy

Mood

We've partnered with professional clinicians to determine the best aspects of well-being people should track. After a several days of tracking the actions that you believe that correlates with one aspect of your well-being, you will receive a result with a graph that visualizes the degree of correlation.
